2021-07-20 Some Summer Activity Suggestions


Here are some summer activity suggestions for college students from the website blog.campusgroups.com .
  • Learn a new skill to add to your resume, including skills outside of your major.
  • Volunteer! It's another bullet on your resume and it can be personally rewarding.
  • Plan a budget for the upcoming school year. Learn to manage your money.
  • Work on any of your weaknesses. For example, improve your writing or public speaking skills.
  • Email your past professors to say thank you or ask for recommendation letters or opportunities.
  • Get a head start on upcoming courses!
  • Get to know your advisor.
  • Start a blog or some other project.
  • Research a topic you would like to explore.
  • Polish your LinkedIn account.
  • Exercise! Get outside! Travel!
