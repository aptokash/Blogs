2021-12-05 Advice and Tips for Finals Week


Here are some tips for studying for finals.
  • Organize your study space to ensure it is comfortable, has good lighting, and has enough space for your books and materials.
  • Create a schedule and game plan for how you will study for multiple exams
  • Find your most productive time for studying
     
  • Plan on getting enough sleep - cramming all night can be counter-productive
  • Take care of your body. Eat 'brain food' rather than junk food.
  • Take regular study breaks. Short study sessions work better than long crams.
     
  • Find study buddies and study groups. Learn by teaching others.
  • Take advantage of professor's office hours!
  • Find a tutor.
     
  • Focus on understanding the material rather than rote memorization.
  • Review your notes, as well as previous test, quizzes, and assignments.
  • Re-read the book, highlighting the more challenging material .
  • Use visual aids, graphs and charts.
  • Use flash cards to regularly test your knowledge
     
  • Stay motivated.
  • Don't forget to reward yourself for all your hard work!

And here's a tip from the book "Your Brain on Music": Neurological research has also found that memory strength is a function on how much we care about the material. The more you care about the material, the better you brain will retain it!
