2021-06-21 Your Knowledge & Skills Portfolio


Computer Science is an ever-changing - and rapidly changing - field. The skills you learn today may be obseolete in a few years! According to the book "The Pragmatic Programmer" by David Thomas & Andrew Hunt, we should all set regular goals to build and maintain our "knowledge and skills portfolio". They recommend:
  • Learn at least one new programming language a year
  • Read a technical book each month
  • Read nontechnical books, too
  • Take classes
  • Participate in local user groups and meetings
  • Experiment with different environments (Windows, MACOS, Linux, IDE's, etc.)
  • Stay current!
