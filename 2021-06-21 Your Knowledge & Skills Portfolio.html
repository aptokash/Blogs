2021--6-21 Your Knowledge & Skills Portfolio

Consider working on your CompSci skills. According to the book "The Pragmatic Programmer" by David Thomas & Andrew Hunt, we should all set regular goals to build and maintain our "knowledge and skills portfolio". They recommend:

  • Learn at least one new programming language a year
  • Read a technical book each month
  • Read nontechnical books, too
  • Take classes
  • Participate in local user groups and meetings
  • Experiment with different environments (Windows, MACOS, Linux, IDE's, etc.)
  • Stay current!
